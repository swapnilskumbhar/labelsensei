# Project: LabelSensei Landing Page

## 1. Product Identity
- **Name:** LabelSensei
- **Tagline:** The Master of the Label. The Verdict on your Health.
- **Role:** An AI-powered "Intelligent Health Inspector" reachable via WhatsApp.
- **Mission:** Providing blunt, clinical assessments of supermarket products (Groceries, Hygiene, Prepared Meals) via label photos.

## 2. Visual & Brand Direction (2026 Modern Aesthetic)
- **Style:** "Zen-Tech" / Modern Minimalism. High-trust, professional, and uncompromising.
- **Color Palette:** - Primary: Transformative Teal (#008080) or Deep Forest Green (Trust & Safety).
    - Background: Soft "Paper" White or a high-end Dark Mode (Charcoal/Navy).
    - Accents: Neon "Safety" Green (for high ratings) and "Alert" Red (for toxic verdicts).
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or Sora) for body; bold, authoritative headers (Playfair Display or Montserrat).

## 3. Website Structure (Single Page Scroll)
### Section 1: Hero (The Hook)
- **Headline:** Stop Guessing. Start Knowing.
- **Sub-headline:** Send a photo of any grocery or personal care label to LabelSensei on WhatsApp. Get a blunt health verdict in seconds.
- **CTA:** [Primary Button: "Start Your First Inspection" (WhatsApp Icon)]

### Section 2: How It Works (The 3-Step Master)
1. **Snap:** Take a clear photo of any product label or ingredients list.
2. **Send:** Message it to the LabelSensei WhatsApp bot.
3. **See:** Receive an instant Rating (1-10) and a "Sensei's Verdict" on safety and quality.

### Section 3: The "Contextual Logic" (Food vs. Hygiene)
- Create two side-by-side cards or a toggle:
    - **Food/Consumables:** Focus on UPFs (Ultra-Processed Foods), hidden sugars, and additives.
    - **Hygiene/Personal Care:** Focus on endocrine disruptors, sulfates, and skin toxicity.

### Section 4: Live Sample (Interactive Mockup)
- Show a WhatsApp chat bubble mockup featuring a product scan (e.g., a popular shampoo or snack).
- Include the "Verdict" card we designed:
    - *Product:* Brand Name
    - *Rating:* 3/10
    - *Verdict:* Contains hidden carcinogens. Avoid daily use.
    - *Analysis:* (The bulleted technical dive)

### Section 5: The "Why LabelSensei?" (Feature Grid)
- **Blunt Honesty:** No marketing fluff. Just the chemical facts.
- **Dual Expertise:** Expert logic for both internal nutrition and external hygiene.
- **Zero Friction:** No app to download. No account to create. Just WhatsApp.

### Section 6: Footer
- Links: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, "Intended Use" disclaimer (Medical/Infant Formula guardrail).
- "Built for the health-conscious shopper."

## 4. Technical Specs for Agent
- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) or Astro.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS.
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first (90% of users will visit from WhatsApp links).
- **Components:** Use Lucide-react icons and Framer Motion for subtle "Sensei" scroll reveals.
