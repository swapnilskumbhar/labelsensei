# Project: LabelSensei AI Landing Page

## 1. Product Identity & Persona
- **Name:** LabelSensei AI
- **Primary Tagline:** The Master of the Label. The Verdict on your Health.
- **Hero Headline:** Stop Guessing. Start Knowing.
- **Role:** An AI-powered "Intelligent Health Inspector" delivered via WhatsApp.
- **Mission:** Providing blunt, clinical assessments of supermarket products (Groceries, Hygiene, Prepared Meals) via label photos.
- **Tone:** Clinical, fast, authoritative, and uncompromising.

## 2. Visual & Brand Direction (Zen-Tech 2026)
- **Style:** "Zen-Tech" / Modern Minimalism. High-trust, professional, and sophisticated.
- **Color Palette:**
    - **Primary:** Transformative Teal (#008080) or Deep Forest Green (Safety/Trust).
    - **Background:** High-end Dark Mode (Deep Navy/Charcoal) or soft "Paper White."
    - **Accents:** Neon "Safety" Green (Ratings 8-10) and "Alert" Red (Ratings 1-3).
- **Typography:** Bold, authoritative headers (Montserrat/Playfair Display) paired with clean sans-serif body text (Inter/Sora).

### Section 1: Hero (The Hook)
- **Headline:** Protecting you, one scan at a time.
- **Sub-headline:** Send a photo of any grocery or personal care ingredients, prepared meals to LabelSensei on WhatsApp. Get a blunt health verdict in seconds.
- **CTA:** [Primary Button: "Start Your First Inspection" (WhatsApp Icon included)]

### Section 2: The 3-Step Master (How It Works)
1. **Snap:** Take a clear photo of any product label or ingredients list.
2. **Send:** Message the image to the LabelSensei WhatsApp bot.
3. **See:** Receive an instant Rating (1-10) and a "Sensei's Verdict" on safety and quality.

### Section 3: Contextual Logic (Food vs. Hygiene)
Display two side-by-side interactive cards or a toggle:
- **Food & Consumables:** Analysis of hidden sugars, UPFs (Ultra-Processed Foods), and harmful additives.
- **Hygiene & Personal Care:** Analysis of endocrine disruptors, sulfates, parabens, and skin irritants.

> **Product:** [Brand Name Shampoo/Snack]

## 4. Technical Specs for Agent
- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) or Astro.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS.
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first (90% of users will visit from mobile).
